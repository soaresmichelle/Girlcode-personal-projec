ABOUT PREMIUM-FARM

Premium Farm is a digital Agriculture Platform that empowers rural farmers and providing them with a market for the sale of their farm produce.

This gives the farmers the capacity to farm more since launching in 2019, Premium farm has empowered over 25,000 small scale farmers across Nigeria.

We are committed to expanding our reach in order to continue to expand local farmers,positively impact their lives and their families while boosting food production ,food marketing and sustainablity in Nigeria.
